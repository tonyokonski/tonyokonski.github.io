About me; basically.

I became interested in web development about 4 years ago while I was working as a communications coordinator in a computer science department at a well-known university. My job enabled me to play with snippets of html and css, and I was amazed at what I could create with them. I was surrounded by computer scientists and their students, and was able to learn from many of them about the basics of web development and programming in general. For a more detailed story of my journey, visit my in-depth bio page.
